Parody sites

Welcome! this website is dedicated to making parody versions of existing website styles.

Page 1: The average News Article

Basically, it's a pretend version of a lot of online news sites these days. It isn't the best, but that's beacuse I don't know how to make it like they do; I'm just me.

Page 2: The Cookbook

Basically, my take on those "cookbook" sites that tell you "here's how to make this good sounding food!" and then, before you can actually get to the instructions, you have to scroll through their 2000 page 'about me' since that's definitally why you're on that site, right?

Page 3: The unfinished homework

A homework assignment that isn't done. You might recognise it.

Page 4: The finale.

The final page, where I tell you about the stuff I learned in this class.
